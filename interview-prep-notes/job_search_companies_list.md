## 🎯 Company Selection Criteria

### **What Makes a Good Target:**
✅ **Series B+ funding** (stable, can pay ₹25L+)
✅ **Product company** (not service)
✅ **Bangalore/Hyderabad** (easier to interview/relocate)
✅ **Uses Ruby/Kafka/Redis** (tech stack match = easier interviews)
✅ **Known for good engineering culture**

### **Red Flags to Avoid:**
❌ Service companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro) - pay similar to Copart
❌ Early-stage startups (Seed/Series A) - risky, low pay
❌ Companies in funding trouble (Byju's, Unacademy) - layoffs
❌ Poor Glassdoor reviews (toxic culture)
